## Key Insights
- Tried evenly-spread columns (draw_spread) but unequal item counts per row made it look terrible. Simple left-aligned with separators is better.
- Budget chart always uses time-x; in non-time mode other charts use turn-x. Mixing these in VLine drawing causes auto-bounds explosion (chart expands to include x=28M minutes, data becomes invisible, hover clears, repeat = flashing).
- TurnInfo.ts_x is always time-minutes regardless of mode; TurnInfo.x is mode-dependent. Budget hover matching must use ts_x in non-time mode.
- Tooltip positioning for budget: no room above or below the small budget chart without covering it. Anchoring tooltip left of usage_rect was the solution.
- CRITICAL LESSON: do not remove or replace working behavior while implementing something new. Flag risk before changing existing features. (Broke cursor-following tooltip while trying to fix click targeting.)
